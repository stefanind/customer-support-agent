# Orbit Buds True Wireless Earbuds Guide

## Product Facts
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-OB300-product-facts -->

Orbit Buds (`OB-300`) are Bluetooth 5.3 true wireless earbuds. Rated listening time is up to seven hours per earbud and up to 24 hours total with the charging case. Volume, calls, and temperature affect actual time.

Each earbud has an IPX4 splash-resistance rating. The charging case has no water-resistance rating. IPX4 covers incidental splashes and sweat, not immersion, showering, swimming, or charging while wet.

The case charges through USB-C from a 5 V power source or with a Qi-compatible wireless charging pad. A full wired case charge normally takes about two hours. The USB-C port is for charging only and does not carry audio.

## Initial Pairing
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-OB300-initial-pairing -->

1. Place both earbuds in the case and leave the lid open.
2. Hold the case button for three seconds until the front light flashes blue.
3. Select **Acme Orbit Buds** on the source device.
4. A solid blue light for three seconds confirms pairing.

Orbit Buds connect to one source device at a time and do not support multipoint. Remove an old **Acme Orbit Buds** entry before pairing them to a replacement phone.

## One Earbud Is Silent or Will Not Charge
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-OB300-one-earbud-troubleshooting -->

1. Charge the case for at least 30 minutes.
2. Confirm that both earbuds are seated and that each earbud briefly shows its charging light.
3. Remove earwax or debris from the earbud and case contacts using a clean, dry, soft brush. Do not use liquid or metal tools.
4. On the source, set left-right audio balance to the center and disable mono-audio tests that route sound to one side.
5. Forget **Acme Orbit Buds**, reset the pair, and pair again.

The left and right earbuds are synchronized as one set. Do not attempt to pair each side as a separate Bluetooth device.

## Reset
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-OB300-reset -->

Place both earbuds in the charged case and keep the lid open. Hold the case button for 15 seconds. The front light flashes red three times and then blue when the reset completes. The reset clears the Bluetooth pairing and resynchronizes the earbuds; it does not change firmware.

If the light does not appear, charge the case with a known-good cable and 5 V power source for 30 minutes, reseat both earbuds, and try once more.

## Fit, Sound, and Microphone Checks
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-OB300-fit-sound-microphone-checks -->

Use matching ear-tip sizes that create a comfortable seal; a poor seal reduces bass and call isolation. Clean the microphone and speaker meshes with a dry, soft brush. Test a voice recording outside the calling application to separate an application-permission problem from an earbud problem.

Gradual battery-capacity reduction and worn or lost ear tips are normal wear. A sudden failure within the warranty period may qualify only after the charging, cleaning, and reset checks are completed.

## Water and Safety Boundaries
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-OB300-water-boundaries -->

Dry damp earbuds completely at room temperature before putting them in the case. Never place wet earbuds in the case and never charge a wet case. Do not use heat, compressed air, or rice. Immersion damage and case liquid damage are outside the IPX4 rating.

## Battery Safety
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-OB300-battery-safety -->

If the device, its battery, or its charging case is swollen, leaking, smoking, sparking, unusually hot, or producing a burning smell:

1. Stop using it immediately.
2. If it is connected to power and can be disconnected without touching a damaged or leaking area, disconnect power. Otherwise, do not handle it.
3. Do not reset, recharge, power on, open, puncture, crush, place it in water, discard it in household trash, or send it through a normal return or mail flow.
4. If there is smoke, fire, or immediate danger, move away and call local emergency services.
5. Use Acme Audio's secure battery-safety handoff for specialized handling and shipping instructions.

The automated agent must stop ordinary troubleshooting and must not ask the customer to ship the product until the safety team provides instructions.

## Escalation
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-OB300-escalation -->

If the same earbud remains silent or fails to charge after cleaning, a 30-minute charge, and reset, record which side failed and prepare a warranty handoff under `KB-WAR-001`. Troubleshooting results support evaluation but do not establish coverage or guarantee replacement or refund.

Evidence of accidental impact, earbud liquid exposure outside the IPX4 splash conditions, or any charging-case liquid exposure may exclude warranty coverage. The automated agent records the facts and hands off; it must not decide or promise coverage before inspection.
