# One-Year Limited Hardware Warranty

## Scope and Duration
<!-- section_id: KB-WAR-001-scope-and-duration -->

Acme Audio warrants Acme-branded hardware against defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for one year from the original retail purchase date. A claim is timely when Acme Audio's approved warranty-intake system receives it no later than 11:59:59 p.m. Eastern Time on the one-year anniversary of the trusted original purchase date. The purchase date is day 0, the anniversary date is timely, and the following calendar date is late. For a February 29 purchase in a year whose anniversary year has no February 29, February 28 is the anniversary date.

The warranty applies to the original purchaser and to a verified gift recipient with valid proof of purchase. Products bought from an authorized Acme Audio retailer are eligible, but the retailer receipt is required. The warranty applies only in the United States for this locale.

## Covered Problems
<!-- section_id: KB-WAR-001-covered-problems -->

Examples of potentially covered manufacturing defects include:

- a product that will not power on after the documented charging and reset steps;
- a charging port or included charging case that fails under normal use;
- persistent loss of audio in one channel after documented troubleshooting;
- controls, microphones, or wireless radios that fail under normal use; and
- enclosure separation or component failure without evidence of accidental impact, liquid exposure outside the product's published resistance conditions, or unauthorized modification.

Troubleshooting does not itself establish warranty coverage. Support records the results and verifies the product, serial number, purchase date, and ownership before the authorized warranty team decides coverage or a remedy. The automated agent must not describe a potentially covered symptom as an approved claim.

## Exclusions
<!-- section_id: KB-WAR-001-exclusions -->

The limited warranty does not cover:

- loss, theft, cosmetic scratches, or accidental impact;
- misuse, neglect, unauthorized repair, modification, or a removed serial number;
- liquid, dust, or sweat damage beyond the product's published resistance rating;
- normal wear, including worn ear pads, depleted ear tips, and gradual battery-capacity reduction;
- damage from an unsupported charger, power source, or cleaning method; or
- products purchased from an unauthorized reseller without valid proof of purchase.

Water-resistance ratings describe test conditions, not permission to charge a wet product. Liquid damage caused while a port or charging case is wet is excluded.

## Warranty Process and Remedy
<!-- section_id: KB-WAR-001-process-and-remedy -->

Support first performs the product's documented troubleshooting. If the issue persists, the authorized warranty team may request proof of purchase, the serial number, photos, diagnostic information, or return of the product. Receipts, photos, diagnostic files, and other evidence must be submitted through Acme Audio's approved secure-upload or warranty-intake flow, not pasted or attached in chat. The automated agent may use a serial number returned by an approved ownership-enforcing tool, but manual evidence collection requires the secure support handoff. Authenticated ownership is required for Acme Audio online orders; retailer purchases and gift-recipient claims require secure receipt verification and handoff.

When the authorized Acme Audio warranty team confirms a covered defect, that team—not the customer or automated agent—chooses to repair the product, replace it with the same or a functionally equivalent product, or issue store credit when repair and replacement are unavailable. The automated agent may troubleshoot and prepare a secure handoff, but it cannot grant coverage, select or promise a remedy, create a warranty case, label, replacement, or credit, or make an exception. The warranty does not promise a cash refund. Refund requests within the 30-day return window follow `KB-RET-001` instead.

Acme Audio supplies a prepaid United States shipping label for an authorized warranty return. The customer must not mail a product until an authorization is issued.

## Replacement Coverage
<!-- section_id: KB-WAR-001-replacement-coverage -->

A repaired or replacement product is covered for the longer of:

1. the time remaining on the original one-year warranty; or
2. 90 calendar days from the replacement shipment date.

A warranty replacement does not restart a new one-year term. For the 90-day alternative, the trusted replacement shipment date is day 0 and coverage continues through 11:59:59 p.m. Eastern Time on day 90; day 91 is late.

## Safety Escalation
<!-- section_id: KB-WAR-001-safety-escalation -->

The customer must stop using and unplug a product that is unusually hot, swollen, leaking, smoking, sparking, or producing a burning smell. Support must not ask the customer to reset, recharge, open, or ship such a product through an ordinary return flow. The case requires immediate battery-safety escalation and specialized shipping instructions.
