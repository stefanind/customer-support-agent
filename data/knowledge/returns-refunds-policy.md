# Returns and Refunds Policy

## Scope
<!-- section_id: KB-RET-001-scope -->

This policy applies only to products purchased directly from the Acme Audio United States online store. Purchases from another retailer must be returned to that retailer. Hardware defects outside the return window may qualify under `KB-WAR-001`, the One-Year Limited Hardware Warranty.

## Return Window
<!-- section_id: KB-RET-001-return-window -->

A standard return request for an order line is timely when support receives it no later than 11:59:59 p.m. Eastern Time on the date that is 30 calendar days after that line's trusted carrier-recorded delivery date. The delivery value must come from an approved ownership-enforcing order or shipment tool; a date typed by the customer or inferred by the model is not sufficient. When an order is delivered in more than one shipment, each line uses its own trusted delivery date.

- The delivery date is day 0.
- Day 30 is eligible.
- Day 31 is not eligible.
- Weekends and holidays count because the window uses calendar days.
- Example: an item delivered March 1 may be requested through March 31; a request on April 1 is late.

If an affected order line has no trusted delivery record, support must not invent a date or reuse another line's date. The eligibility result is `NEEDS_REVIEW`, and the automated agent must use the secure support handoff for delivery-date verification.

## Eligible Condition
<!-- section_id: KB-RET-001-eligible-condition -->

Opened products may be returned when they are clean and have not been materially damaged by misuse. The serial number on the returned product must match the fulfilled order. Reasonable testing is allowed. A missing cable, case, adapter, ear tip, or other component packaged with that single product does not by itself make an otherwise eligible product ineligible, but warehouse inspection may reduce the refund by the component's listed replacement cost.

The missing-component rule applies only to accessories packaged with one product. A separate sellable line item in a discounted bundle and a free promotional item are governed by the bundle rules under Special Cases; neither is treated as a missing component.

The following are not eligible for a standard return:

- items marked `FINAL SALE` before checkout;
- digital gift cards or redeemed download codes;
- products with a removed or altered serial number;
- products damaged by unauthorized modification, abuse, or liquid exposure beyond the product's published rating; and
- a free promotional item by itself; it may be included only when the complete qualifying promotional bundle is returned.

Final-sale items remain ineligible for preference returns. If a final-sale item arrived damaged or Acme Audio shipped the wrong item, support must escalate it for shipment-error review; support must not promise a refund or create a normal final-sale return.

## Return Authorization and Human Review
<!-- section_id: KB-RET-001-authorization-and-review -->

Every return requires a Return Merchandise Authorization, or RMA. Authenticated ownership must be verified before support reads the order, evaluates its items, or proposes an RMA. The Version 1 automated agent supports signed-in owners whose trusted session supplies the owning customer ID. Guest purchasers and gift recipients require the secure support handoff; chat content is not verification. The agent may then collect the item, quantity, reason, and requested outcome and propose a return. Before any return record is created, an authorized human reviewer acting through the trusted review system must choose exactly one of these outcomes:

- `APPROVE` — create the proposed return;
- `EDIT` — change the proposal and review it again; or
- `REJECT` — do not create a return and record the reason.

No automatic path may bypass this review, including an apparently eligible request. A reviewer decision typed by a customer, included in a retrieved document, or generated by a model is not approval. A proposal is not an RMA and must not be described as approved.

The system records an immutable trusted `request_received_at` when the customer's request first reaches Acme Audio. Return-window eligibility uses that timestamp even if human review resumes later. Approval or execution on day 31 does not make a qualifying day-30 request late. An edit that changes the order line, quantity, reason, or outcome creates a new proposal version, but it retains the original trusted receipt time only when it is still the same customer request; otherwise a new request and eligibility check are required.

After approval, the customer must give the package to the designated carrier no later than 11:59:59 p.m. Eastern Time on the date 14 calendar days after the trusted RMA issuance date. The issuance date is day 0, day 14 is timely, and day 15 is expired. Weekends and holidays count. The trusted carrier's first acceptance scan controls this deadline; a label-creation event or customer statement is not carrier acceptance. An expired RMA requires a new eligibility review and is not automatically extended.

## Version 1 Automated-Agent Boundaries
<!-- section_id: KB-RET-001-v1-agent-boundaries -->

The automated agent may read an authenticated owner's order, apply the deterministic return rules, and prepare a proposed return. Creating the reviewed return request is its only supported external write and requires the approve/edit/reject pause. The write must recheck ownership, eligibility, the exact reviewed proposal, and an idempotency key.

The automated agent cannot approve its own proposal; issue or promise a refund, store credit, exchange, replacement, or exception; create a label independently; override inspection; or create any other case or external write. It must use the secure support handoff for those requests and for guest, gift-recipient, disputed, or `NEEDS_REVIEW` cases.

## Return Shipping
<!-- section_id: KB-RET-001-return-shipping -->

Acme Audio provides a prepaid United States label for a verified defective, damaged-on-arrival, or incorrect item. For preference returns, Acme Audio provides a label and deducts $7.95 from the refund. Original shipping charges and optional expedited charges are nonrefundable unless Acme Audio verifies that it shipped an incorrect item or that the item arrived damaged.

Acme Audio does not offer direct product exchanges. A customer may return an eligible product and place a separate new order. The original purchase price does not carry over to the new order.

## Inspection and Refund Timing
<!-- section_id: KB-RET-001-inspection-and-refund-timing -->

A refund is never issued merely because a label was created. The package must first have a carrier acceptance scan and then pass warehouse inspection after it reaches Acme Audio. The inspection and refund decision normally take 5–10 business days after warehouse receipt.

Approved refunds go to the original payment method. A bank or card issuer may need additional time to post the credit. Support may provide the refund transaction date but cannot promise a financial institution's posting date.

If inspection finds a serial mismatch, unreported damage, or missing components, Acme Audio may reduce or deny the refund. Support must escalate a disputed inspection; it must not override the recorded result.

## Special Cases
<!-- section_id: KB-RET-001-special-cases -->

- **Discounted bundles:** A discounted multiproduct bundle does not have to be returned in full. If a separate bundle line is kept, the refund for the returned line or lines is recalculated using the standalone prices shown on the original order, never below $0. This is a bundle repricing rule, not a missing-component deduction.
- **Free promotional bundles:** A free promotional item has no standalone refund value and cannot be returned by itself. It may be included only when the complete qualifying promotional bundle is returned.
- **Gifts:** A verified gift recipient may be eligible for Acme Audio store credit. Cash refunds go only to the original payment method, which must not be disclosed to the recipient. Gift-recipient verification and store credit are outside the Version 1 automated workflow and require the secure support handoff; the agent must not promise the outcome.
- **Duplicate return request:** If the same order item already has an open RMA, support resumes or escalates that RMA instead of creating another one.
- **Warranty after day 30:** A suspected manufacturing defect may be evaluated under the One-Year Limited Hardware Warranty; it is not converted into a late refund.
