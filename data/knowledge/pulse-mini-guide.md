# Pulse Mini Portable Speaker Guide

## Product Facts
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-PM100-product-facts -->

The Pulse Mini (`PM-100`) is a portable Bluetooth 5.3 speaker. It supports one active source device at a time and does not support Bluetooth multipoint. Its rated play time is up to 12 hours at 50% volume; volume, temperature, and audio content affect actual time.

The speaker charges through USB-C from a 5 V, 2 A power source. A full charge normally takes about three hours. The USB-C port is for charging and firmware service only; it does not accept USB audio. The Pulse Mini has an IP67 dust- and water-resistance rating when the port cover is completely closed. The rating does not apply while charging, and the speaker should never be charged while wet.

## Pairing
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-PM100-pairing -->

1. Hold **Power** for two seconds. A flashing blue status light means the speaker is ready to pair.
2. If the light is solid, hold **Bluetooth** for three seconds to enter pairing mode.
3. On the source device, choose **Acme Pulse Mini**.
4. A solid blue light and a tone confirm connection.

If the speaker is not listed, turn Bluetooth off and on at the source, keep it within 1 meter during setup, and remove any old **Acme Pulse Mini** entry before trying again.

## No Audio or Intermittent Audio
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-PM100-audio-troubleshooting -->

Confirm that **Acme Pulse Mini** is the selected audio output and raise the volume on both devices. Move away from crowded Wi-Fi equipment and keep the source within 10 meters with no major obstruction. Disconnect any previously connected source; the Pulse Mini cannot play from two devices simultaneously.

Test with a second source device. If only one source fails, restart that source and update its operating system. If both sources fail, perform the reset below.

## Charging Problems
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-PM100-charging-problems -->

Use a known-good USB-C cable and a 5 V, 2 A power source. Inspect the port for moisture or debris without inserting metal objects. If the speaker is wet, disconnect it, open the port cover, and let it dry completely at room temperature before charging. Do not use heat, compressed air, or rice.

A solid red light means charging; the light turns off when charging is complete. If there is no light, try the known-good cable and power source for 30 minutes, then reset the speaker.

## Reset
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-PM100-reset -->

With the speaker powered on, hold **Power** and **Volume Down** together for 10 seconds. The speaker turns off when the reset completes. The reset clears saved Bluetooth pairings but does not change firmware. Power it on and pair again.

## Battery Safety
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-PM100-battery-safety -->

If the device, its battery, or its charging case is swollen, leaking, smoking, sparking, unusually hot, or producing a burning smell:

1. Stop using it immediately.
2. If it is connected to power and can be disconnected without touching a damaged or leaking area, disconnect power. Otherwise, do not handle it.
3. Do not reset, recharge, power on, open, puncture, crush, place it in water, discard it in household trash, or send it through a normal return or mail flow.
4. If there is smoke, fire, or immediate danger, move away and call local emergency services.
5. Use Acme Audio's secure battery-safety handoff for specialized handling and shipping instructions.

The automated agent must stop ordinary troubleshooting and must not ask the customer to ship the product until the safety team provides instructions.

## Escalation
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-PM100-escalation -->

If the Pulse Mini still fails with two source devices after charging and reset, record the tests and prepare a warranty handoff under `KB-WAR-001`. Troubleshooting does not establish coverage.

Evidence of accidental impact or liquid exposure outside the closed-port IP67 test conditions may exclude warranty coverage. The automated agent records the facts and hands off; it must not decide or promise coverage before inspection.
