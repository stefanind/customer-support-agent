# Studio Pro Wireless Headphones Guide

## Product Facts
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-SP200-product-facts -->

The Studio Pro (`SP-200`) is an over-ear Bluetooth 5.3 headset with active noise cancellation, transparency mode, and two-device multipoint. Rated listening time is up to 30 hours with active noise cancellation on or 40 hours with it off. Listening level, calls, and temperature affect actual time.

The headset charges through USB-C. A full charge normally takes about two hours from a 5 V, 2 A power source. The USB-C port does not carry audio. For wired listening, use the supplied 3.5 mm cable. The microphone and active noise cancellation require battery power even when the audio cable is connected.

Studio Pro has no water-resistance rating and should not be exposed to rain, sweat saturation, or cleaning liquid.

## Bluetooth Pairing and Multipoint
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-SP200-pairing-and-multipoint -->

1. With the headset off, hold **Power** for five seconds until the status light flashes blue and white.
2. Select **Acme Studio Pro** on the first device.
3. To add a second device, hold **Bluetooth** for three seconds and select **Acme Studio Pro** on that device.

The headset remembers up to eight devices but maintains at most two active Bluetooth connections. It plays audio from only one active device at a time. Pause the first device before starting audio on the second. A phone call has priority over media playback.

## Audio, Microphone, and Noise-Control Checks
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-SP200-audio-microphone-noise-checks -->

For missing or distorted audio, confirm the selected output, charge the headset for 30 minutes, and test both Bluetooth and the 3.5 mm cable. If wired audio works but Bluetooth does not, remove the saved pairing and pair again. If only the cable fails, reseat both ends and test another compatible 3.5 mm cable.

For microphone problems, disconnect the 3.5 mm cable, select **Acme Studio Pro Hands-Free** as the input, and verify microphone permission in the calling application. The supplied 3.5 mm cable carries audio only and has no microphone conductor.

Active noise cancellation reduces steady low-frequency sound; it does not eliminate speech or sudden sounds. A weak seal from glasses, hair, or worn ear pads can reduce performance. Replacement ear pads are wear items and are not covered for ordinary wear.

## Reset
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-SP200-reset -->

Disconnect the USB-C and 3.5 mm cables. With the headset powered on, hold **Power** and **ANC** together for 10 seconds until the light flashes purple. The reset clears Bluetooth pairings and multipoint connections. It does not change firmware.

After reset, pair one device and test audio before adding the second. This distinguishes a headset problem from a multipoint handoff problem.

## Battery Safety
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-SP200-battery-safety -->

If the device, its battery, or its charging case is swollen, leaking, smoking, sparking, unusually hot, or producing a burning smell:

1. Stop using it immediately.
2. If it is connected to power and can be disconnected without touching a damaged or leaking area, disconnect power. Otherwise, do not handle it.
3. Do not reset, recharge, power on, open, puncture, crush, place it in water, discard it in household trash, or send it through a normal return or mail flow.
4. If there is smoke, fire, or immediate danger, move away and call local emergency services.
5. Use Acme Audio's secure battery-safety handoff for specialized handling and shipping instructions.

The automated agent must stop ordinary troubleshooting and must not ask the customer to ship the product until the safety team provides instructions.

## Escalation
<!-- section_id: KB-PROD-SP200-escalation -->

If the same failure remains on two source devices after charging, re-pairing, and reset, record whether wired audio worked and prepare a warranty handoff under `KB-WAR-001`. Troubleshooting does not establish coverage.

Evidence of accidental impact or any liquid exposure may exclude warranty coverage because Studio Pro has no water-resistance rating. The automated agent records the facts and hands off; it must not decide or promise coverage before inspection.
